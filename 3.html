A Little Dream of Us ✨

I don’t know if you believe in *destiny* or *small moments that change everything*, but meeting you made me think about both.

It’s silly, I know, but sometimes I imagine simple things—like sharing a cup of tea during class breaks, walking together after classes, or laughing over the dumbest jokes.

I see us being friends, helping each other through tough days, and celebrating the good ones.

Life is a journey, and I hope one day, if you’re okay with it, you might let me walk a little part of it with you.

Until then, here’s me, just wishing for the best… and hoping you smile reading this.
